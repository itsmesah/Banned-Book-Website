About Me: A Blend of Pixels and Prose

Hey there! I'm Sahar, a lover of both the written word and the digital world. I'm all about merging my two passions: literature and tech. By day, I tinker with code, and by night, I'm lost in worlds spun from sentences, working on my very own short novel. It's a balancing act between the logic of programming and the free flow of storytelling, but I wouldn't have it any other way.

I'm on a mission to become a published author. That's the dream. To see my name on a book cover, to hold the weight of my words in printed form—there's nothing quite like it. But it's not just about getting my own stories out there. I'm also here to take a stand against the censorship that keeps so many voices silent.

This project you've stumbled upon? It's not just for a grade. It's a platform that I'm building with the skills I've honed, a project that will grow and adapt, just like the tech it's built on. I want to create a space where literature can be free from constraints, where banned books find their audience, and where open discussions can flourish.

So, whether you're here to discover hidden literary gems or to explore the intersection of literature and technology, welcome. Let's make sure that every story gets its chance to be told, and that the only thing that gets censored is censorship itself.
